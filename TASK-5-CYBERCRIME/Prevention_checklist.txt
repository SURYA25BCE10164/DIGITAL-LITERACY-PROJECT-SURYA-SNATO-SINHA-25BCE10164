STAY SAFE ONLINE 

Stay Safe Online – Checklist for College Students (India)
Use strong, unique passwords for each account and enable two-factor authentication (2FA) wherever possible.
Avoid sharing personal details (phone number, address, college info) publicly on social media.
Think before posting—once online, content can be permanent and widely shared.
Do not click on unknown links or download attachments from unverified sources.
Verify profiles before interacting—fake accounts are commonly used for scams and harassment.
Regularly update apps and devices to protect against security vulnerabilities.
Use privacy settings on platforms like Instagram, WhatsApp, and LinkedIn to control who can contact or view you.
UPI & Financial Safety Tips:
Never approve unknown UPI collect requests or scan QR codes to receive money—this is a common scam.
Do not share OTP, UPI PIN, or bank details with anyone, even if they claim to be from a bank.
If something goes wrong:
Report cybercrime at: https://cybercrime.gov.in
Call the Cyber Crime Helpline: 1930 immediately for financial fraud cases
